Nursing Internship

A nursing internship abroad is a voluntary healthcare placement undertaken in a busy yet low-resource hospital in the developing contries. You can choose which departments you want to get experience in. Whether your focus is adult, pediatrics, mental health or otherwise, you can totally customize your nursing student internship. You’ll see fascinating cases like tropical infectious diseases, see how sociocultural issues impact the delivery of care, and get real-life insight into global health. If you’re interested in spending time in laboring wards, and pre and postnatal departments we can create a specialised internship for you.

REQUIREMENTS

As long as you have started on a professional nursing degree you are eligible.you can also travel you are already graduated.

Physical Therapy internship

A physical therapy internship abroad is your chance to do a clinical placement in a low-resource hospital in the developing world. You’ll see a huge difference between the local healthcare system and what you’re used to. You’ll also learn about practices you’ve never encountered before — electrotherapy and wax baths for example. You’ll see uncommon conditions like clubfoot and spina bifida, and learn how limited resources affect the care provided

REQUIREMENTS

As long as you have started on a professional physical therapy degree you are eligible.You can also travel if you are already graduated.

Paramedicine internship

An paramedicine internship is a placement undertaken in a low-resource, high-patient volume hospital in the developing world. You’ll see just how different from your own a healthcare system can be. And you’ll see conditions and practices you’ve never encountered. It’s worth noting that paramedics and ambulance services are luxuries of the developed world. Your role in our destinations is focused more on emergency medicine. You’ll see how local staff deal with emergency cases with limited resources, learn how cultural issues affect how care is delivered (there’s less of a sense of urgency in some destinations), and see practices that you just won’t see at home, like manual intubation.

REQUIREMENTS

As long as you are enrolled on a Associate of Applied Science Degree in Paramedicine or you’re currently a National Registered Paramedic you are eligible to travel with us.

Midwifery Internship

A midwifery internship is a voluntary hospital placement you undertake in under-resourced wards in east africa. Our internships are the perfect complement to your midwifery or nursing studies.on this programme you will get experience in prenatal and postnatal care, labor wards…. Whether you’re studying towards a professional midwifery or nursing degree, your volunteer experience is completely customizable. You’ll will also be in a position to see labor complications resulting from a lack of prenatal care, how limited funding impacts care provided (many women labor without pain-relief), and learn about cultural beliefs surrounding childbirth.An interniship will help you expand on your knoeledge develop confidence,build interpersona skills and,sharpen your communications skills and stand out from nursing and midwifery students.the placement runs from Monday to Friday,this gives you the oppotunity to spend your evennings and weekends travelling around the country.

REQUIREMENTS

As long as you have started your professional nursing or midwifery degree you are eligible.you can also travell when you have already graduated.

Radiology

You’ll see just how different a healthcare system can be from your own, while getting experience with practices and conditions you’ve never seen. You choose which destination you want to travel to and let us know your clinical interests. We’ll then completely customize your radiology internship program to suit you. You’ll help scan for and diagnose unfamiliar conditions, learn techniques like developing X-rays on film, and see how cultural factors affect the manner in which local staff go about their practice.This internship placement will help you expand your knowledge and interpersonal skills and sharpen your communication skills.

REQUIREMENTS

As long as you have started your professional radiology or radiologic technology degree program you are eligible.you can also travel if you already graduated.
